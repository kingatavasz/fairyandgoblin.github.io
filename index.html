Fairy and Goblin - Adventures in the Forest and Beyond

About the Book

A rose fairy who loves beautiful clothes, a charmingly polite and chivalrous little goblin, a letter from an oak-wraith, a fierce battle against aphids... A guided tour inside a giant oak, a journey on the wings of the wind in a leaf boat, life inside a rose, air-bubble elevator inside a tree-trunk... The stories of Zilla and Thorn transport you to a world of tiny creatures full of their adventures. These charming tales for preschoolers and small schoolchildren sensitively portray emotions familiar to us all, human relationships, and characters that invite identification while also highlighting the values and inimitable beauty of nature that need to be preserved.

About the Author

Kinga Tavasz is a storyteller and nature enthusiast who weaves tales of wonder for young children.
