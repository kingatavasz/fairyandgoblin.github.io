Fairy and Goblin

Adventures in the Forest and Beyond

Kíra the unicorn – a magical friend in the forest

A rose fairy who loves beautiful clothes, a charmingly polite and chivalrous little goblin, a letter from an oak-wraith, a fierce battle against aphids... A guided tour inside a giant oak, a journey on the wings of the wind in a leaf boat, life inside a rose, air-bubble elevator inside a tree-trunk...

The stories of Zilla and Thorn transport you to a world of tiny creatures full of their adventures. These charming tales for preschoolers and small schoolchildren sensitively portray emotions familiar to us all, human relationships and characters that invite for identification while also highlighting the values and inimitable beauty of nature that need to be preserved.
